About Coder Dojo

The Raspberry Pi Foundation is a UK-based charity that aims to enable people of all ages to explore computing and develop digital skills. They are headquartered in Cambridge, England, and have made a significant impact with their affordable Raspberry Pi computers, which are used in education and industry worldwide.

The foundation's mission is to put the power of computing into the hands of people all over the globe and help them harness it to shape a better future.

For more details, visit their official website: raspberrypi.org.
